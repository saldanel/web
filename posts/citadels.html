Citadels recommendations

Citadels can be hard when carrying the wrong army.

Citadel Catapults Recommendation
Elven L10100 tier2Only ranged troops
Elven L15400 tier3Only mounted troops
Elven L20800 tier4Only melee troops
Elven L253200 tier5Only melee troops
Elven L306400 tier6Only melee troops
Cursed L202200 tier4Only melee troops
Cursed L257800 tier5Only melee troops

Griffins are welcome on any of them, and usually is a good idea to carry Captain Brann to increase the catapults efficiency.

Catapults equivalence between tiers is 2. That means you can multiply by 2 and carry lower level catapults or divide by 2 if you carry higher level catapults. Examples:

Tier2 Tier3 Tier4 Tier5 Tier6
1005025137
80040020010050
32001600800400200
8800440022001100550
2560012800640032001600
64400312001560078003650

Rememer these calculus are aproximate and it is always better to carry on with a bit more catapults if you are not using the recommended ones.

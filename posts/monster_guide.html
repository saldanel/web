Monster Guide

This is the army recommendations I personally suggest for every single kind of monsters.

Simple guide

The rule is simple:

  • Against Ranged, use Mounted
  • Against Mounted, use Melee
  • Against Melee, use Ranged

And avoid the kind of units the enemy is specialized against.

Common

The recommended ones will attack first more frequently.

For instance, Undead level 1 are Melee units, so if you use recommended Ranged, you will always attack first. If you try using Melee, the attack order might depend on the amount of units. If you use disallowed Mounted units, then you will probably attack the last and will lose more units due to the bonifications.

Avoid Catapults and Spies in any case.

Flying units are always recommended unless the opposite is said.

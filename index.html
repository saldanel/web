Saldanel the TotalBattle fan site

I'm Saldanel, your host. Welcome to my site, where I expect you can find useful advise on your daily TotalBattle battles. I could have been Kathrirana, Taucage or any other name generated by the game or I could have modified it by Mystica, Elros, Ghost, ... , but it choosed Saldanel for me, and I liked it.

TotalBattle is a real time colaborative game, representing a middle age world plenty of might and magic. Each player rules a city with a Hero and several Captains. The game complexity grows as long as you get more experience, what provides a good evolution for the game. The better you understand the game, the better player you will be.

And that's why I created this site: to help you and me to improve the game experience, by providing some advises or just a "to do" list to get the most of each event.

This is a live site and will change over time. If you have any constructive suggestion, find a bug or just want to contact me, I'm available at saldanelsilver@gmail.com

Advises for today (2025-09-05)

Remember to take the daily prize (500 Gold):

  • Browser/TotalBattle
  • Browser/Triumph
  • Mobile (IOS/Android)

Participate on the Lucky Bazar as soon as possible, in order to try to take advantage of benefits in Experience and Value Points benefits it provides. Then, try to get all the prices by killing monsters to get movement benefits which could last the whole event if renewed. When possible, spend your coins in the Bazar 10 by 10, what will increase the probability of getting more coins.
Usually these links might give you some extra coins:

  • Browser/TotalBattle
  • Browser/Triumph
  • Mobile (IOS/Android)

You might get advantage of VIP and Bonos for Experience, Value Points and Army Strength when attacking Tinman.

Carrying mercenaries on your battles will give you extra power, so bu tuned on the channels where players publish the location for Mercenaries Bags. But be quick to get them or the bag will sell out! Usually it is a good idea to use Tengel with the Drum to pick up them.
